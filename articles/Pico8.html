Pico8

Pico-8 is a fantasy console that mimics commodore-64 style games and is written in Lua. 2 projects have been made, witchery a zelda-like dungeon crawler showcasing AI-states, inventory systems and 2D game development. The game features 3 bosses, various enemy types and puzzles. (Github) and (Itch.io)

Another project made is City-8 a simple city builder based off of popular city builders like City Skylines or Simcity with a building selection menu a adpactive road placment system where the shape of the road changes depending on the adjacent roads to connect them together and simple game logic to calculate city growth/decline, happiness and income (Github) and (Itch.io)
