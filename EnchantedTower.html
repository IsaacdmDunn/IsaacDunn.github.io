A rogue-like game currently under development with 3 other people in Unreal Engine with me being the Game Programmer/ Developer. Art by Dawid Michalski, Design by Harkirit Gill and Story/ Writing by Sam Hall. The game will be a rogue-like about and father and daughter attampting to escape a tower with you playing as either character and the other one joining and aiding you in battles with items, enemies and procedurally generated rooms.
