Forecaster

How to use it

Forecaster can be broken down into 2 sections, Historical and Forecasting. Historical mode will show past data from jan 1996 - aug 2022 and can be navigated using the calender on the top left.
Forecasting mode can be activated using the forecasting button and will show the next 12 months of data predicted.
